Makhani Chicken (Indian Butter Chicken)

The butter chicken is one of spicy non-veg dishes. It can be made as mild or spicy as you wish by adjusting the cayenne. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

Ingredient

  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil, divided
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • ¼ white onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala, divided
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ¼ cup water

Method

  1. Cut chicken fillet to bite-sized cubes
  2. Marinate chicken in tandoori paste for about 30mins.
  3. In a large, heavy-based saucepan, heat ghee on medium. Cook chicken 4 to 5 mins, turning frequently to sear well. Remove from pan
  4. Using the same pan, add tomato paste, ginger, chilli, spices and sugar. Cook, continuously stirring for 1 to 2 mins
  5. Add water, then cream. Bring to simmer on medium heat
  6. Return chicken to sauce and simmer on medium heat for 15 to 20 mins, until chicken is cooked through
  7. Add lemon juice and season to taste, stir well
  8. Serve with naan, mango chutney and lemon wedges. Enjoy!
